About Us

Stanna Agencies is a trusted importer and distributor of high-quality glass in Sri Lanka over a period of 50 years, since 1972. We provide a wide range of glass types, including Float, Tempered, Laminated, and Frosted Glass, catering to construction, interiors, and design needs. Our commitment is to quality, competitive pricing, and timely delivery to all our clients.
